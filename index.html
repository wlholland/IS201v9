Mountain Biking

i.e. How to find greater joy in life

Why Mountain Bike?

  • Full-body Workout: Mountain biking is great for strengthening muscles and improving cardiovascular health.
  • Outdoor Adventure: It offers the thrill of exploring nature and discovering new trails.
  • Stress Relief: Riding in nature reduces stress levels and promotes mental well-being.
  • Social Connection: It can be a fun activity to enjoy with friends or meet fellow cyclists.
  • Personal Achievement: Overcoming challenges on the trail boosts confidence and self-esteem.

Why Mountain Bikes are better than Girlfriends (for men)

  • You can spend time with your bicycle without it nagging or criticizing you. In fact, it will stay relatively quiet most of the time.
  • Bicycles live for the moment – they don’t think or ask about what the “next step” in the relationship is.
  • Related to the previous, is that your bike won’t rehash old issues – you don’t have to worry about being “surprised” with stories of your past offenses.
  • Bikes won't get upset if you forget to call or text them. Bikes can't talk, or use a phone.
  • Bikes won't get upset if you decide to go for a ride with someone else.
  • Bikes are generally ready to go whenever you’re ready to go. There’s no need to wait around for them.

Why Mountain Bikes are better than Boyfriends (for women)

  • It won't leave the toilet seat up (bikes don't need to use the bathroom)
  • Bikes won't complain about your driving skills—they'll just roll with it.
  • Bikes won't forget your anniversary and then try to make up for it with last-minute flowers.
  • You can spend time with your bike and it won't be constantly checking sports updates on their phones
  • They won't keep you up all night because of their snoring. Bikes don't snore, and they stay in the garage!
  • Bikes won't complain about your cooking, or how long it takes for you to do your makeup (We love mountain bikes!)
